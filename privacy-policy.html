Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Claude Content Bot ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to publish content to TikTok.

2. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with the App via TikTok Login Kit, we collect the following information through the user.info.basic scope: your TikTok display name, username, and profile picture. This information is used solely for account verification purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to authenticate your TikTok account and publish content on your behalf through the Content Posting API. We do not use your information for any other purpose, including marketing or advertising.

4. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the device running the App. No user data is stored on external servers or in cloud databases.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only transmitted to TikTok's API as required for authentication and content publishing.

6. Data Retention

We retain your authentication tokens only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, which will invalidate all stored tokens.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, request deletion of your data, revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time, and contact us with any privacy-related concerns.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at gadgetlabsuk@gmail.com.
